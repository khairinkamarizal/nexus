Writing Mechanics

Do's

  • Use Sentence Case for all headlines, buttons, and navigation (e.g., "Sign up," not "Sign Up").
  • Be concise. If you can say it in three words, don't use five.
  • Focus on the user. Use "You" and "Your" more than "We" or "Our."

Don'ts

  • Don't use tech jargon like "hash," "node," or "distributed ledger" unless the user is an admin.
  • Don't blame the user. Avoid "You entered the wrong password." Use "Password not recognized."
  • Don't mix button styles. Stick to the designated verb-noun structure (e.g., "Save Changes").
